PillPal

PillPal is a smart robot that reminds patients to take their medications. It does this with an email, SMS and Cloud-based recording for caregivers. Once reminded, simple to follow instructions are presented. Once taken, the patient can confirm consumption and are presented with the time of their next dose, preventing overdose.
